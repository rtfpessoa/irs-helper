# How IRS Helper Works

This section explains the process of tax declaration enrichment in three simple steps:

adasdasda

## Step 1: Upload XML File
You start by uploading your official tax declaration XML file. This provides the structure and required fields for the tax authority.

## Step 2: Upload PDF Reports (Gains & Dividends)
Next, upload all relevant PDF reports from your brokers (XTB, Trade Republic). The tool automatically parses these documents to extract structured tables containing your financial transactions (gains/dividends).

## Step 3: Enrichment and Download
The application combines the data. It intelligently maps the extracted transaction rows into the correct fields of your XML declaration. You can then use the **Diff Viewer** to confirm accuracy and download the enriched file for filing.
